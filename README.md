# 🪐 Sports Astral Forecast Engine

> **An Advanced Astrology-Powered Sports Intelligence & Forecasting Platform**

Sports Astral Forecast Engine is a next-generation research platform that combines **Vedic Astrology**, **Krishnamurti Paddhati (KP)**, **Western Astrology**, **Astronomical Ephemeris**, and **Vedic Numerology** to study and forecast the energetic conditions surrounding sporting events.

Unlike conventional prediction systems that rely primarily on historical statistics, machine learning, or betting models, this platform focuses on the **astronomical and astrological signatures** associated with sporting events, venues, teams, captains, and match timings. It integrates precise planetary calculations with location intelligence, numerology, muhurat analysis, and environmental data to generate a comprehensive astrological assessment.

The project is designed as a modular, extensible framework suitable for researchers, astrologers, developers, and sports enthusiasts interested in exploring celestial influences on competitive events.
